Site Report

My Journey Towards Web Development

Our journey is our own, and it is filled with lots of challenges as well as opportunities. Starting my coding journey through Python has been a great experience so far.
While diving into the world of Web Development, everything seemed so intimidating. However, as I progressed, the art of web design was what I truly desired.

Basic Learnings

Starting with the fundamentals, I learned to structure the webpage through HTML. The use of correct tags and elements is really important for the proper functioning of the website.
When I moved to CSS, it was even more exicting experience because the power CSS holds is just wonderful. Stylying and transforming a basic HTML into an art is what i loved more. Although it is very hard to manage everything.
Still it is worth all the hassle.

Design and Debugging

Designing the website's interface was very challenging as well as satisfying. I experimented with various colors and finally chose my theme colors. I went to all black with a dash of yellow. Black because everyones loves darkmode and to add a pop of soothing color I used yellow.
I just took some concepts on how should I do this from diffent youtube channels and finally created my whole new different project.
Among all this debugging was not that difficult then I was expecting. Everything went smoothly just faced a few problems with my layout inconsistency and fewer tags problems.

Design Decision

For the font, I chose (arial, sans-serif), because I found this very simple and clean looking which increases its readability. I chose my images to be in circle shape. For the projects, I placed an empty placeholder which I can modify in accordance with future projects. I attached my photo with the background image because of which it sticks around with the background.

Reflective Decision

As this was my first ever project to work with all these codes and web designs, learning through every process, I realized how much I have grown in understanding web development.All of my confusion found a way out in pretty beautiful way through style. This particular practical project helped a lot in solidifying my learnings.
One of my major priority was to make this website user-friendly and very easily accessible, and I tried my best to do so. Here I learned the importance of user-centered designs. Passing through a lots of planning and testing, I was able to achieve this.
